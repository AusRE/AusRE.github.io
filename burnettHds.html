Burnett Heads

These photos where taken at Burnett Heads which is the port on the Burnett river for the city of Bundaberg.

  • Yacht club
  • Home for the Naval Cadets
  • Marina
  • Seafood Resturants
  • Whale watching tours
  • Barrier Reef tours to Lady Musgrave Island and Lady Elliott Island
  • Go fishing on the charter boats or just fish of the banks or the retaining walls
  • Boat ramps for your own boat to go fishing up the river or out to the reefs
  • Go for walks along the seashore back to Bargara via Mon Repos turtle sanctuary
